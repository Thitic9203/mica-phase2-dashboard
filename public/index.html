To ensure accurate tracking, please keep the following up to date in Jira:
  • Epic — every ticket must be linked to its corresponding epic so it is correctly attributed to its system.
  • Sprint — assign a sprint to every active ticket; unassigned tickets are excluded from all charts and statistics.
  • Status — reflect the real-time progress of each ticket (e.g. In Progress, Ready to Test, Done).
  • Assignee — indicate who is currently responsible for the ticket on the development side.
  • QA Owner — assign the QA member responsible for verifying and signing off on each ticket.
